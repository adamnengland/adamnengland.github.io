Hi Adam,

I am a Video Acquisition Editor at Packt Publishing. We're currently coming up with a short video course on Lo-dash. Would you be interested in developing this course?

Our Packt Video courses provide viewers with practical, friendly screencast tutorials on interesting and emerging technologies. Although Packt is best known for its technical books, Video is currently the fastest-growing part of the business. We hope you’ll work with us to further expand our catalog. We pay authors a royalty on all copies sold. With the knowledge and enthusiasm you have for the topic, past experience in creating tutorials is ideal but not essential. You just need to be able to present the material in clear and friendly spoken English.

If you like the idea of being a published video author or have any questions or ideas, please reply to this mail and let me know. I look forward to hearing from you.
