Logo and tagline are less important than you think. Monty Python used a foot. Additional funding can fix a logo. It can't fix a bad or improperly executed vision

Love Open Source - Monty Python used a public domain song. Could they have spent money on a custom song? Possibly. However, it probably wouldn't have been a better song than Sousa could write, and it would have taken focus away from their product - Comedy. Don't let concerns about support and "enterprise-readiness" distract you. When you have a killer product, someone will always be willing to sell you support.
