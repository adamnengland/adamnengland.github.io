So, we got an adorable Romo to live at our house. His name is Slar, after the gorn from Star Trek. Yeah.

The tutorials and lab are fun, but what I'm really interested in is developing an app using the SDK. To get started, romotive has documentation here http://romotive.com/developers-quickstart/, but lets start out slow and see what we get.

Fire up Xcode

1. Start from the HelloRomo project

Delete the romo folder and then drag the 2 frameworks & 1 bundle into the frameworks in xcode

If you're only using RMCore:
Drag frameworks/RMCore.framework into your app's frameworks folder.
In additon, go to the "Build Phases" tab for your app's target, and add the following external frameworks:
CoreMotion.framework
ExternalAccessory.framework
If you're only using RMCharacter:
Drag frameworks/RMCharacter.framework and frameworks/RMCharacter.bundle into your app's frameworks folder.
In additon, go to the "Build Phases" tab for your app's target, and add the following external frameworks:
AVFoundation.framework
QuartzCore.framework
